The Elden Leaf: A Tale of Salad in the Lands Between

In the shadowed corners of the Lands Between, where the Erdleaf sprouts and ancient castles crumble into dust, there existed a once-vibrant kingdom known as Greenshade.
It's people worshipped the Great Garnish, a divine relic said to be the source of all vitality and flavor in the world.
The Great Garnish, an eternal, shimmering lettuce leaf, was said to be plucked from the hands of the Salad Elden, a god of taste and balance.
But as with all things in the Lands Between, peace was not eternal.
The Fracture of Dressing, a cataclysmic event caused by the Great Garnish's corruption, split Greenshade into warring factions, each claiming their own right to wield the power of the Great Salad Bowl.
Chaos ensued. The balance of crunch, zest, and dressing was forever lost.

The Garnished Leafbearer

You are Garnished, a lowly wanderer, exiled from the glory of Greenshade.
You awaken in the Field of Wilt, surrounded by rotting lettuce and shattered croutons.
A faint glow pierces through the mists-a light guiding you toward the forgotten Shrine of the Elden Dressing, where the secrets of the Great Garnish may yet linger.
A voice calls to you. It is Chef Oloric, a spectral figure draped in aprons of tarnished gold.
His eyes are weary, but his voice is steady. “Tarnished Leafbearer, long have you slumbered. The balance of salad has been lost.
